About Us

Welcome to the TinSnip Hairoom thanks for checking us out

Hair has been my profession, passion & craft for many years now. Being fortunate enough to have created this space where I can share all the things I love being surrounded in with you. Using what I believe is the best sustainable Vegan haircare to ensure hair health & a unique hair experience.

"The TinSnip Hairoom" is a dream come true! A mid 60's Coronet caravan transformed into a moroccan, bohemian, gypsy, eclectic hair studio in a quiet Brisbane suburb surrounded by birds, trees & nice open spaces with sea breezes.

It's great to be able to offer services that are:

Vegan

All our products and services are 100% Vegan. This means our products do not contain any animal ingredients or animal-derived ingredients.

100% Organic

All our products and services are 100% Certified organic.

Locally Australian Made

Our products such as Everescents Organic Haircare & Hot Tresses are locally Australian made!

Safe & Nontoxic

Our luxurious Professional nontoxic colour range is Certified Organic Natulique. Ammonia Free, Pesticide Free, Sustainable, Plant Based, Vegan, Eco Friendly.
